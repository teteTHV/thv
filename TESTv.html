About Taehyung

Kim Taehyung, known as V, is a multi-talented artist from BTS. Celebrated for his deep, resonant voice and artistic vision, Taehyung blends jazz, pop, and indie in his solo work, like his debut album Layover. His love for photography, cinema, and Paris inspires his dreamy aesthetic.
